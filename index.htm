The mastermind of the team. The IGL that focus more on the game than his score. He wants to make so his clutch players win the round, his entrys to get those entry kills and make his awper a threat to the enemies.

Shaun

Pontus "Shaun" Petersson

The deadly AWPer. The AWP in his hands is a match made in heaven but he can also play the AK or M4. When he has his AWP the enemy is going to have a hard time to even breath when he is nearby. He can be anywhere thats the thing that make him so strong as a player. He will look for you, he will find you and he will kill you.

Bempan

Bempan is a very focused player and all he wants is to make his team victorious. Bempan has the support role in Hygoe, he is also 2rd awper in Hygoe. Bempan is a very skilled player that's mostly win the clutch situations.

Anton.K

Anton "Anton.K" Källman

The beast. Give him any gun and he will frag with it. When he has a good game you dont want to be on the enemy team. When he is rushing a site he is unstoppable. Molotov, Smoke, Grenade? He will rush through it and kill you. You will regret that you were born.
